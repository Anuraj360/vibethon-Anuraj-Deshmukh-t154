Beginner: What is ML?

Building the foundation of Artificial Intelligence.

🤖 The Simple Explanation

Imagine you want to teach a computer to identify a fruit. Instead of writing rules like "if it's red and round, it's an apple," you show the computer thousands of pictures of apples. The computer learns the patterns itself. That's Machine Learning.

Real-world examples:

  • Spam Filters: Learning which emails you delete to protect your inbox.
  • Netflix: Learning your taste to suggest your next favorite show.

🧩 Types of Learning

Supervised

Learning with a "teacher" using labeled data (e.g., photos labeled "Cat").

Unsupervised

Learning without a teacher, finding hidden patterns in unlabeled data.
